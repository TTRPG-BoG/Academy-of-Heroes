Place of Origin: Mycenae (known for its warriors and fortifications)

Strengths:
Wealth, Might, Fortitude, Presence

Behavior:
Naturally competitive and determined to prove himself in physical challenges.

Family:
Father: Damocles (wealthy merchant) – A demanding man, focused on expanding his trade empire.
Mother: Cleo (former soldier, now a homemaker) – Stern but caring.
Younger Sister: Phaedra – Leandros adores her and feels protective of her.
Uncle: Timon (former Academy graduate, deceased) – Suffered a fatal wound defending Mycenae.
